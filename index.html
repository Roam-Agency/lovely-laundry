FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is your turnaround time?

Standard turnaround is typically within a few working days, and we offer an express service when you need items back faster — often within 24 hours. For doorstep collection through The Ironing Man, items are usually returned within 24 hours.

What are your hours of operation?

Our Stockton store is open Monday to Thursday 8:30am–5:00pm and Saturday 9:00am–1:00pm. You can book a doorstep pickup online any time.

Do you do collection & delivery?

Yes — through The Ironing Man, our group's doorstep service. Schedule a pickup online or in the app, we collect, clean and deliver back to you. Book a pickup →
